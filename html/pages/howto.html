How to Play

Welcome!

Objective

Your main objective is to manuver the four (red, green, blue, and yellow) robots such that one of them
ends up ontop of the exit icon (purple dot).

Rules

The rules are as follows:
-You may only move one robot at a time.
-Robots continue to move until they colide with an object (i.e. a wall or another robot).
-Robots may slide off the board if not stopped by an object.
-Robots that slide off the board are lost for that run.

Winning

The player only wins when a robot has successfully stopped ontop of the exit marker (purple dot).
Simply sliding across it does not count. The robot MUST STOP on top of the purple dot.
